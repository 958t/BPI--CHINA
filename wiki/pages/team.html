Character Introduction

Wang Ruili-Team leader

Wang Ruili,As a technical backbone,she has participated in and completed more than 10 projects such as the National 863 Young Scientist Topic, the National Key Research and Development Pathogen Special Project, and the Beijing "Science and Technology New Star" Program.At present, he has published more than 4 SCI and EI papers, declared 4 invention patents and authorized 2 projects. In recent years, he has guided students to participate in various skills competitions and innovation and entrepreneurship competitions, and won 1 gold medal of Beijing Challenge Cup, 1 second prize and 3 third prizes of Beijing "Internet +" College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition.

Character Introduction

Chen Liang-Team leader

Graduated from Beijing University of Chemical Technology with a doctorate degree in chemical engineering and technology, he is a senior engineer in the field of measurement control and instrumentation of the Chinese Instrumentation Society, a senior technician of chemical inspectors, a senior technician of agricultural products and food inspectors, a coach (senior) of the International Entrepreneurship Association, a lecturer of KAB entrepreneurship education (China), an academic leader of Beijing Electronic Science and Technology Vocational College, a winner of the Youth May Fourth Medal, and a top talent in Beijing. He serves as the standing director of the Industry-Education Integration Professional Committee of the China Education Development Strategy Society, the standing director of Beijing Modern Manufacturing Vocational Education Group, the Fine Chemical Professional Committee of the National Chemical Industry Vocational Skill Appraisal Technical Committee and the expert of the Professional Construction Expert Committee of the National Inspection Testing and Certification Vocational Education Group.

Character Introduction

Wang Cuicui (Higher Vocational Education) -Team leader

Lecturer of Beijing Polytechnic, Director of Class 2 of Class 2022 Pharmaceutical Biotechnology. She is mainly responsible for the teaching of 《Biochemistry》、《Gene Manipulation Technology》and《Microbial Technology》.She is active in genetics and plant molecular genetics research fields. She has published 7 scientific papers, participated in the publication of 1 book and applied for 2 invention patents. She hosts two school-level research projects, and participates in 1 project of Beijing Municipal Education Commission.
