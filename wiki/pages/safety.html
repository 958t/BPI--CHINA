laboratory

    BPI-CHINA always prioritizes the safety of experiments. On this page, we will demonstrate how we continuously implemented various measures to ensure the safety and security of all related parts, from experiments to future application. Just like our predecessors, before starting our wet lab work, all members of BPI-CHINA wet team members have received laboratory safety training from our experimental director. Our project were carried out mainly in the laboratory classified as biosafety level 1. The equipment used in the laboratory, work space, reagent management are all in accordance with the relevant rules of iGEM safety.

The picture shows the safety items found in our biotechnology laboratory.

Team Safety

  • Always know the correct procedure for the disposal of lab materials.
  • Fully consider the experimental process, and make full preparations.
  • Safe and reasonable use of water and electricity, close as needed.
  • Keep the lab tidy and hygienic.
  • Tie back loose or long hair.
  • Wear long-sleeved lab coats in and out of the lab.
  • Always use gloves and splash-proof goggles.
  • All contaminated waste must be disinfected before treatment.
  • Do not carry all items in the laboratory outside.
  • Do not eat or drink when in the lab area.
  • Wash hands coming in and out of the laboratory.
  • Take photos and supervise every time the laboratory is cleaned.

Project Safety

overview

    Our project has developed test strips that are easy to prevent, detect myocardial infarction diseases. Specifically, our project modifies gene fragments with different hairpin structures on colloidal gold, targets different miRNAs, binds to miRNAs and initiates HCR thermostatic amplification reactions.Meanwhile, the reverse cleavage function of CRISPR technology was used to release the fluorescence signal and complete the miRNA detection. This project will combine colloidal gold technology, HCR thermostatic amplification technology and CRISPR reverse cleavage to establish a specific method for detecting miRNAs, and predict whether patients have myocardial infarction according to the test results.

hardware
